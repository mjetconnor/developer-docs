Spigit Communities

Spigit communities wear two hats when it comes to the SpigitGraph data model.  Some aspects of Spigit communities are exposed as Community entities and their properties.  Other aspects are exposed as SuggestionBox or Challenge entities and their properties.

In Spigit terms, there are "regular" and "challenge" communities.

Regular communities appear in SpigitGraph as Community entities.  The communities property of the tenant entity contains all of the communities in the tenant that are accessibly to the currently authenticated user.  Regular communities may form parent-child relationships with each other, forming a hierarchical tree, as represented by the parent and children properties of the Community entities.

Community entities have an inquiries property.  The value of this property lists all of the inquiries contained within the community.  The first such inquiry in every community is a SuggestionBox.  This entity represents that aspect of the Spigit community where ideation takes place, where ideas are posted directly to the community.

Spigit challenge communities appear in SpigitGraph as Challenge entities within their parent community's  inquiries property, usually following the initial suggestionBox that is associated with every community.  Note that these challenge communities are not represented as Community entities but instead as Challenges.  Challenges are not included in the tenant's list of communities.  They only appear in the parent community's list of inquiries.

The main difference between suggestionBoxes and challenges is that challenges have a time structure, start and end dates and sometimes phases, whereas suggestionBoxes are open-ended.  Beyond that, most of the data and function of these two kinds of inquiries are the same.

Spigit communities may be configured to have special idea templates, idea stages, and idea graduation criteria.  These values are all exposed in the SpigitGraph data model as properties in the relevant inquiry, and repeated as properties on individual idea entities.  Idea templates may be found in the uiForm property of inquiries and ideas.  Idea stages may be found in LifeCycle entities.
